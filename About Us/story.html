Our Story

Benilda Anderson


Hi, I'm Benilda Anderson, founder of Ben's Whisk & Wonder. We are a delightful bakery specializing in custom cakes and pastries for all your special occasions. Our passion lies in creating beautiful, delicious treats that make every celebration memorable

Founded in 2010, Ben's Whisk & Wonder started as a small home-based bakery driven by my love for baking and creativity. What began as a hobby quickly grew into a thriving business, thanks to the support of our wonderful community and loyal customers. Over the years, we have expanded our offerings and honed our craft, always striving to deliver exceptional quality and service.

At Ben's Whisk & Wonder, we believe that every cake tells a story. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, wedding, anniversary, or any other milestone, our skilled bakers and decorators work closely with you to bring your vision to life. From classic designs to whimsical creations, we cater to all tastes and preferences.

We use only the finest ingredients to ensure that every bite is a perfect blend of flavor and texture. Our menu features a wide variety of cakes, cupcakes, cookies, and pastries, all made fresh daily. We also offer gluten-free and vegan options to accommodate diverse dietary needs.

Visit us at Ben's Whisk & Wonder and let us help you make your next celebration truly special with our exquisite baked goods. Your sweet dreams are our command!
